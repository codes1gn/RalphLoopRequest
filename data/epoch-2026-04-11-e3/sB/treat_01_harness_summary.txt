1. checkpoint_cli.py
   Purpose: CLI harness for durable-request checkpoint testing without a live
   AskQuestion UI. It simulates presenting options and receiving an auto-chosen
   response, records timing/metadata in CheckpointResult, and exposes subcommands
   for single calls, transcript verification (file or stdin), batched runs, and
   a scripted test suite across task contexts (code, debugging, analysis, etc.).
   Transcript verification uses regex to ensure **Completed**, numbered options,
   a Done-style option, and the freeform "Or just tell me what to do next:" line.
   The test-suite subcommand prints grouped results and writes JSON to
   /tmp/durable-verify/results/harness-test-suite.json.
